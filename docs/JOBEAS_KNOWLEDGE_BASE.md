# About Jobeas

**Jobeas** helps job seekers create strong **resumes** and **cover letters**, prepare for **interviews**, and move through **job search and application** workflows in one place. We combine easy-to-use tools with optional **AI assistance** so you can draft, refine, and tailor your materials faster—while staying in control of what you submit.

Jobeas is offered online through **jobeas.com** (and related official pages). Use only our published site and apps when signing in or sharing personal information.

---

## What you can do

- **Build and edit resumes** using professional layouts (templates) suited to different styles and industries.
- **Upload or optimize** existing resume content where those features are available in your account.
- **Create cover letters** tailored to roles you are applying for, and keep copies you can revisit.
- **Explore job-related tools** such as listings, application tracking, and **interview preparation**, depending on what is enabled for your account.
- **Download or export** your materials when the product supports it (for example, PDFs), so you can use them outside Jobeas.

Exact features may vary by plan or region; where in doubt, what you see in your account after signing in is the source of truth.

---

## How we use AI

Jobeas may use artificial intelligence to **suggest wording**, **summarize**, **reorganize**, or **optimize** your resume and cover letter based on what you provide. AI output is meant to **assist** you—not to replace your judgment. You should **review and edit** anything before you use it in an application.

AI features can make mistakes or reflect biases present in training data. Jobeas is **not** a law firm, career counseling service, or human recruiter. For high-stakes decisions, consider professional advice.

Details about data use appear in our **Privacy Policy** (see below).

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Jobeas free?**  
We may offer a free tier or trial alongside paid plans. Check the Pricing area on the site for current options.

**Do you store my resume?**  
If you save work while logged in, we store it so you can edit and download it later. See the Privacy Policy for retention and your choices.

**Will AI write my whole resume for me?**  
AI can help draft and improve text, but you decide what to keep. Always verify accuracy (dates, employers, titles, skills).

**Who is Jobeas for?**  
Primarily **job seekers** who want structured tools and AI-assisted writing. Employers or partners should contact us through official channels.

**Can I use Jobeas outputs anywhere?**  
You are responsible for ensuring your final resume and letters meet employer requirements and that you have rights to any content you submit.
