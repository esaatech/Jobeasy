How Gmail Integration Works

1

Secure Connection

We use Google's OAuth 2.0 to securely connect to your Gmail account. We never store your password.

2

Send Emails

When you send a resume or cover letter, it will be sent from your Gmail account to the recipient.

3

Full Control

You can disconnect your Gmail account at any time. We only send emails when you explicitly request it.

Privacy & Security

• We only request permission to send emails on your behalf

• We cannot read your emails or access your inbox

• Your Gmail credentials are never stored on our servers

• You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings
